I just want to see trans girls happy with flash that looks like them. I want to see girls with dicks with tattoos of girls with dicks.

i knew that i wanted to create representation for tgirls in tattooing, that i wanted to fill in the gap in the american traditional style. this idea of turning trad into something that is representative and powerful has stayed with me since i watched a video @flesh_and_fluid put out on creating Black Panther flash. using these references from people who might refuse my existence and appropriating them to be for us is deeply gratifying.

the first two artists i saw making flash that had tgirls in it are @sanyutattoo and @marshobrecker . I am forever grateful that these incredible artists exist, especially to be friends with Sanyu and have their art on my body forever
