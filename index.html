River Arachne is a transfemme multi-media artist, an Editor at TTTism, and is learning to draw tattoos with the help of S. Tamez. Her intention is to make gender affirming tattoos with trans and non-binary people and has been working on an essay/archive entitled Floating Above about non-medical ways to experience gender euphoria. Her interests also include: leather, masochism, transsexual hunger, lavender, pleasurable performance
