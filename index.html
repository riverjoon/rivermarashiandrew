River Marashi Andrew is a transfemme multi-media artist, a TTTism Collaborator, and is learning to draw tattoos with the help of S. Tamez. Her intention is to provide gender affirming tattoos for trans and non-binary people. She recently finished her essay entitled Floating Above and is using image making to document her physical transition. She graduated from Parsons School of Design with a BFA in Communication Design in 2022.
